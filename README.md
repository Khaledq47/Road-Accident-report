# Project objectives
Create road accident report to have insight such as:
1. Total casualties and accident values for 2022 and the change percentage growth comparing to the previous year
2. Total casualties by accident severity for 2022 and the change percentage growth comparing to the previous year
3. Total casualties with respect to vehicle for 2022
4. Monthly trend showing the comparison of casualties for 2002 and 2021
5. Casualties by road type for 2022
6.  Casualties by Area, location and by day/night
7.  Total casualties and accident by location

#Tools Used
Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Time Intelligence functions.
